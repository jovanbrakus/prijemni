\[ 2x + 1 > 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad x > -\tfrac{1}{2} \] \[ 2x + 9 > 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad x > -\tfrac{9}{2} \] \[ 2x - 4 > 0 \quad \Rightarrow \quad x > 2 \]
