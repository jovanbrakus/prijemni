  • Uslov za minimum (izvod): Ako je \( f'(x_0) = 0 \) i \( f''(x_0) > 0 \), tada je \( x_0 \) taƒçka lokalnog minimuma.

\[ 4x + \frac{9\pi^2}{x} \geq 2\sqrt{4x \cdot \frac{9\pi^2}{x}} \]

\[ 2\sqrt{4 \cdot 9\pi^2} = 2\sqrt{36\pi^2} = 2 \cdot 6\pi = 12\pi \]

Korak 3

Jednakost u AM-GM va≈æi kada su oba sabirka jednaka, tj. kada \( 4x = \frac{9\pi^2}{x} \):

\[ 4x^2 = 9\pi^2 \quad \Longrightarrow \quad x^2 = \frac{9\pi^2}{4} \quad \Longrightarrow \quad x = \frac{3\pi}{2} \]

(Biramo pozitivan koren jer je \( x > 0 \).)

üß† Proveri se: Koliko iznosi \( 4x \) za \( x = \frac{3\pi}{2} \)?

\( 4 \cdot \frac{3\pi}{2} = 6\pi \). A \( \frac{9\pi^2}{x} = \frac{9\pi^2}{3\pi/2} = \frac{9\pi^2 \cdot 2}{3\pi} = 6\pi \). Oba sabirka su jednaka \( 6\pi \), pa zbir iznosi \( 12\pi \). AM-GM jednakost va≈æi!

üß† Proveri se: Izraƒçunaj \( f\!\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) \) direktno.

Korak 6

\[ f'(x) = 4 - \frac{9\pi^2}{x^2} + \cos x \]

Za \( x = \frac{3\pi}{2} \):

\[ f'\!\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = 4 - \frac{9\pi^2}{\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right)^2} + \cos\frac{3\pi}{2} \]
\[ = 4 - \frac{9\pi^2}{\frac{9\pi^2}{4}} + 0 = 4 - 4 + 0 = 0 \quad \checkmark \]
Korak 7

Proveravamo da li je ovo zaista minimum pomoƒáu drugog izvoda:

\[ f''(x) = \frac{18\pi^2}{x^3} - \sin x \]

\[ f''\!\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right) = \frac{18\pi^2}{\left(\frac{3\pi}{2}\right)^3} - \sin\frac{3\pi}{2} = \frac{18\pi^2}{\frac{27\pi^3}{8}} - (-1) \]
\[ = \frac{18\pi^2 \cdot 8}{27\pi^3} + 1 = \frac{144}{27\pi} + 1 = \frac{16}{3\pi} + 1 > 0 \quad \checkmark \]

üß† Proveri se: Za≈°to je ovaj lokalni minimum ujedno i globalni?

AM-GM nam daje da je \( f(x) \geq 12\pi - 1 \) za svako \( x > 0 \), i ova vrednost se dosti≈æe, pa je to globalni minimum.
