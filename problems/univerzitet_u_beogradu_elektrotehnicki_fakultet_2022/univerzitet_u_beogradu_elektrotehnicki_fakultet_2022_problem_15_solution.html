Trigonometrijska jednacina sa tangensom i kotangensom

Smena, formula za tangens zbira i kvadratna jednacina sa dvostrukim korenom

Postavka zadatka

Zbir realnih resenja jednacine \(\displaystyle \operatorname{tg}\!\left(x - \frac{\pi}{12}\right) \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\!\left(x + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) = \frac{1}{3}\) na segmentu \([0,\, 2\pi]\) iznosi:

Ponudjeni odgovori

(A)
\(3\pi\)
(B)
\(\dfrac{\pi}{4}\)
(C)
\(\dfrac{5\pi}{4}\)
(D)
\(4\pi\)
(E)
\(\dfrac{3\pi}{2}\)

Plan resavanja

Koristicemo vezu \(\operatorname{ctg}\alpha = \frac{1}{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}\) da transformisemo proizvod u kolicnik tangensa. Zatim primenjujemo formulu za tangens zbira da bismo dobili algebarsku jednacinu po \(t = \operatorname{tg}(x - \frac{\pi}{12})\). Resavamo kvadratnu jednacinu, nalazimo \(x\) na datom segmentu i racunamo zbir.

📐 Kljucne formule i teoreme
  • Veza tangensa i kotangensa: \(\operatorname{ctg}\alpha = \dfrac{1}{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}\), pa je \(\operatorname{tg}\alpha \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\beta = \dfrac{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}{\operatorname{tg}\beta}\)
  • Tangens zbira: \(\operatorname{tg}(\alpha + \beta) = \dfrac{\operatorname{tg}\alpha + \operatorname{tg}\beta}{1 - \operatorname{tg}\alpha \cdot \operatorname{tg}\beta}\)
  • Poznate vrednosti: \(\operatorname{tg}\dfrac{\pi}{6} = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = \dfrac{\sqrt{3}}{3}\)
  • Periodicnost tangensa: \(\operatorname{tg}\alpha = \operatorname{tg}\beta \iff \alpha = \beta + n\pi,\; n \in \mathbb{Z}\)
  • Diskriminanta: Ako je \(D = b^2 - 4ac = 0\), kvadratna jednacina \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\) ima dvostruki koren \(x = \dfrac{-b}{2a}\).

Vizuelni prikaz

Resenje korak po korak

1

Transformacija jednacine

Koristimo vezu \(\operatorname{ctg}\alpha = \frac{1}{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}\), pa je:

\[ \operatorname{tg}\!\left(x - \frac{\pi}{12}\right) \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\!\left(x + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) = \frac{\operatorname{tg}\!\left(x - \frac{\pi}{12}\right)}{\operatorname{tg}\!\left(x + \frac{\pi}{12}\right)} = \frac{1}{3} \]

Odavde sledi:

\[ \operatorname{tg}\!\left(x + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) = 3 \cdot \operatorname{tg}\!\left(x - \frac{\pi}{12}\right) \]
2

Smena argumenata

Neka je \(a = x - \frac{\pi}{12}\). Tada je:

\[ x + \frac{\pi}{12} = a + \frac{\pi}{6} \]

Jednacina postaje:

\[ \operatorname{tg}\!\left(a + \frac{\pi}{6}\right) = 3 \cdot \operatorname{tg}(a) \]
3

Primena formule za tangens zbira

Koristimo \(\operatorname{tg}(a + \frac{\pi}{6}) = \frac{\operatorname{tg} a + \operatorname{tg}\frac{\pi}{6}}{1 - \operatorname{tg} a \cdot \operatorname{tg}\frac{\pi}{6}}\), gde je \(\operatorname{tg}\frac{\pi}{6} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\):

\[ \frac{\operatorname{tg} a + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}}{1 - \frac{\operatorname{tg} a}{\sqrt{3}}} = 3 \cdot \operatorname{tg} a \]

Uvodimo oznaku \(t = \operatorname{tg} a\) radi preglednosti:

\[ \frac{t + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}}{1 - \frac{t}{\sqrt{3}}} = 3t \]
4

Svodjenje na kvadratnu jednacinu

Mnozimo obe strane sa imeniocem \(\left(1 - \frac{t}{\sqrt{3}}\right)\):

\[ t + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = 3t\!\left(1 - \frac{t}{\sqrt{3}}\right) = 3t - \frac{3t^2}{\sqrt{3}} = 3t - \sqrt{3}\,t^2 \]

Preuredimo:

\[ \sqrt{3}\,t^2 - 2t + \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = 0 \]

Mnozimo celu jednacinu sa \(\sqrt{3}\):

\[ 3t^2 - 2\sqrt{3}\,t + 1 = 0 \]
5

Resavanje kvadratne jednacine

Racunamo diskriminantu:

\[ D = (2\sqrt{3})^2 - 4 \cdot 3 \cdot 1 = 12 - 12 = 0 \]

Diskriminanta je jednaka nuli — jednacina ima dvostruki koren:

\[ t = \frac{2\sqrt{3}}{2 \cdot 3} = \frac{2\sqrt{3}}{6} = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{3} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \]
6

Nalazenje ugla \(a\)

Iz \(\operatorname{tg} a = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = \operatorname{tg}\frac{\pi}{6}\) sledi:

\[ a = \frac{\pi}{6} + n\pi, \quad n \in \mathbb{Z} \]
7

Vracamo se na promenljivu \(x\)

Posto je \(a = x - \frac{\pi}{12}\):

\[ x - \frac{\pi}{12} = \frac{\pi}{6} + n\pi \]
\[ x = \frac{\pi}{6} + \frac{\pi}{12} + n\pi = \frac{2\pi}{12} + \frac{\pi}{12} + n\pi = \frac{3\pi}{12} + n\pi = \frac{\pi}{4} + n\pi \]
8

Resenja na segmentu \([0,\,2\pi]\)

Biramo vrednosti \(n\) tako da \(x \in [0,\, 2\pi]\):

\[ \begin{aligned} n = 0: & \quad x_1 = \frac{\pi}{4} \approx 0{,}785 \quad \checkmark \\[6pt] n = 1: & \quad x_2 = \frac{\pi}{4} + \pi = \frac{5\pi}{4} \approx 3{,}927 \quad \checkmark \\[6pt] n = 2: & \quad x_3 = \frac{\pi}{4} + 2\pi = \frac{9\pi}{4} \approx 7{,}069 > 2\pi \quad \times \end{aligned} \]
9

Provera domena i verifikacija

Za oba resenja proveravamo da su tangens i kotangens definisani (argumenti ne smeju biti visekratnici od \(\frac{\pi}{2}\)):

Za \(x_1 = \frac{\pi}{4}\):

\[ \operatorname{tg}\!\left(\frac{\pi}{4} - \frac{\pi}{12}\right) \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\!\left(\frac{\pi}{4} + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) = \operatorname{tg}\frac{\pi}{6} \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\frac{\pi}{3} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = \frac{1}{3} \quad \checkmark \]

Za \(x_2 = \frac{5\pi}{4}\):

\[ \operatorname{tg}\!\left(\frac{5\pi}{4} - \frac{\pi}{12}\right) \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\!\left(\frac{5\pi}{4} + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) = \operatorname{tg}\frac{7\pi}{6} \cdot \operatorname{ctg}\frac{4\pi}{3} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} = \frac{1}{3} \quad \checkmark \]
10

Racunanje zbira

\[ x_1 + x_2 = \frac{\pi}{4} + \frac{5\pi}{4} = \frac{6\pi}{4} = \frac{3\pi}{2} \]

Kljucni uvid

Jednacina \(\operatorname{tg}(\alpha) \cdot \operatorname{ctg}(\beta) = k\) se uvek svodi na kolicnik \(\frac{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}{\operatorname{tg}\beta} = k\), sto daje linearnu vezu izmedju dva tangensa. Kada su argumenti oblika \(x \pm c\), razlika argumenata je konstantna (\(2c\)), pa se primenom formule za tangens zbira dobija kvadratna jednacina. U ovom slucaju, diskriminanta je tacno nula — to znaci da postoji jedinstven ugao (do na period \(\pi\)), sto je elegantan rezultat. Dva resenja na \([0, 2\pi]\) su tacno za \(\pi\) razmaknuta, pa je njihov zbir uvek oblika \(2x_0 + \pi\) — ovde \(2 \cdot \frac{\pi}{4} + \pi = \frac{3\pi}{2}\).

Ceste greske

  • Brkanje kotangensa i tangensa: Cesta greska je da se zapise \(\operatorname{ctg}\alpha = \operatorname{tg}\alpha\) umesto \(\operatorname{ctg}\alpha = \frac{1}{\operatorname{tg}\alpha}\). Time se gubi kolicnik i jednacina dobija potpuno drugaciji oblik.
  • Zaboravljanje perioda tangensa: Tangens ima period \(\pi\), a ne \(2\pi\). Ako se koristi period \(2\pi\), propusta se resenje \(x = \frac{5\pi}{4}\), i dobija se samo \(\frac{\pi}{4}\) — odgovor (B), sto je zamka.
  • Pogresno sabiranje razlomaka: Pri racunanju \(\frac{\pi}{6} + \frac{\pi}{12}\), cesta greska je da se dobije \(\frac{\pi}{18}\) umesto \(\frac{\pi}{4}\). Treba svesti na isti imenilac: \(\frac{2\pi}{12} + \frac{\pi}{12} = \frac{3\pi}{12} = \frac{\pi}{4}\).
  • Neprovera domena: Mora se verifikovati da argumenti \(x \pm \frac{\pi}{12}\) nisu oblika \(\frac{\pi}{2} + k\pi\), gde tangens ili kotangens nisu definisani. Za nasa resenja argumenti su \(\frac{\pi}{6}\), \(\frac{\pi}{3}\), \(\frac{7\pi}{6}\), \(\frac{4\pi}{3}\) — svi su u domenu.
