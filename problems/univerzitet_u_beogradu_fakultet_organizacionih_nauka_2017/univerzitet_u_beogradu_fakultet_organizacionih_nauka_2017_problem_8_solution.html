\[ x + 4 > 0 \quad \Longrightarrow \quad x > -4 \]

\[ 4x + 16 > 0 \quad \Longrightarrow \quad x > -4 \]

\[ t = \log_2(x + 4) \quad \Longleftrightarrow \quad x + 4 = 2^t \]

\[ 4x + 16 = 4(x + 4) = 4 \cdot 2^t = 2^2 \cdot 2^t = 2^{t+2} \]

3

\[ \log_{4x+16}\,8 = \log_{2^{t+2}}\,2^3 \]

\[ \log_{2^{t+2}}\,2^3 = \frac{3}{t+2} \]

8
